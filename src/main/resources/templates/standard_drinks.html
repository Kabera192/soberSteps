Standard Drinks Calculator

What is a Standard Drink?

A standard drink is a measure of alcohol consumption representing a hypothetical beverage that contains a fixed amount of pure alcohol. It is a good measure of how much one drinks and helps to compare different types of alcoholic beverages.

How to Calculate a Standard Drink

To calculate the number of standard drinks in a beverage, use the formula:

Volume of Beverage (in Liters) * Percentage of Alcohol in Beverage * 0.789

For example, a 0.3l beer with 5% alcohol has 1.2 standard drinks in it.
